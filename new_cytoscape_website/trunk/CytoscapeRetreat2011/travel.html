Lodging Options

The La Jolla Shores Hotel

  • Hotel Reservations Department at (800) 237-5211.
  • Please reference Cytoscape Conference when making reservations.
  • Reservations must be made before April 8, 2011

The Village

  • $110 per night for a 4 room apartment
  • Various other "dorm-style" options
