COMPONENT_IN_SAME  
Infers an interaction between A and B entities if they belong to the same molecular complex. This does not necessarily mean they directly physically interact. In a complex with n molecules, this rule will create a clique composed of n(n-1)/2 interactions. The inferred interaction is undirected.
COMPONENT_OF  
Infers an interaction between A and C entities if A is a component of C, which is a complex. This interaction is transient in the sense that A component_of B and B component_of C implies A component_of C. The inferred interaction is directed.
CO_CONTROL_DEPENDENT_ANTI  
Infers an interaction between A and B entities if they exert control over the same process, and their control is dependent, i.e. they both affect control of the most downstream process. Also, their effect is in different directions (one of them activates, the other inhibits). The inferred interaction is undirected.
CO_CONTROL_DEPENDENT_SIMILAR  
Infers an interaction between A and B entities if they exert control over the same process, and their control is dependent, i.e. they both affect control of the most downstream process. Also, their effect is in the same direction (both activate or both inhibit). The inferred interaction is undirected.
CO_CONTROL_INDEPENDENT_ANTI  
Infers an interaction between A and B entities if they exert control over the same process, and their control is independent, i.e. they act without affecting each other's activity. Also, their effect is in different directions (one of them activates, the other inhibits). The inferred interaction is undirected.
CO_CONTROL_INDEPENDENT_SIMILAR  
Infers an interaction between A and B entities if they exert control over the same process, and their control is independent, i.e. they act without affecting each other's activity. Also, their effect is in the same direction (both activate or both inhibit). The inferred interaction is undirected.
CONTROLS_METABOLIC_CHANGE  
Infers an interaction between A and B entities if A catalyses a reaction that either consumes or produces B. More formally, this rule will connect two entities A and B if and only if A controls a conversion which B participates and appears only on the left or right side of the conversion but not both. The inferred interaction is directed.
CONTROLS_STATE_CHANGE  
Infers an interaction between A and B entities if A catalyses a reaction that either consumes or produces B. More formally, this rule will find an interaction between two entities A and B if and only if A controls a conversion which B participates and appears both on the left or right side of the conversion. The inferred interaction is directed.
PARTICIPATES_CONVERSION  
Infers an interaction between A and B entities if they both participate in a conversion as substrates or products. Controllers are not included. The inferred interaction is undirected.
PARTICIPATES_INTERACTION  
Infers an interaction between A and B entities if they both participate in an interaction. Controllers are not included. The inferred interaction is undirected.
SEQUENTIAL_CATALYSIS  
Infers an interaction between A and B if they catalyze two conversions that are connected by a common molecule, and where potentially that common substrate is produced by the former and consumed by the latter. The inferred interaction is directed.
