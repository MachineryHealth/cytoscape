We are mainly working on two projects; genome sequence analyses and molecular interaction analyses. For the former analyses, we are especially interested in non-protein-coding regions of various genomes including E. coli and H. sapiens. For the latter analyses, we aim to uncover how the molecules in cells are organized, and to discover novel signal transduction pathways.
