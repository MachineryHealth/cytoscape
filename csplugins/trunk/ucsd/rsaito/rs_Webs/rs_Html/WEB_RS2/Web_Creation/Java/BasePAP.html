Optimal annealing of two RNA sequences

Overview: In this page you can find one of optimal annealings of two nucleic acid sequences based on free energy calculation.

Instruction: All you have to do is to enter two nucleic acid sequences in the text fields below, and press "anneal" button. In a few minutes, you will see annealing of two sequences in the text area below with calculated free energy. If you enter long sequences, you have to wait for a long time to get a result.

Methods: Annealing is determined according to alignment of two sequences. Alignment score is determined by free energy calculation. Free energy is calculated by base pairings and structural energy required to form bulge loops and interior loops(Hairpin loops are not considered).

Energy of base pairings, bulge loops, and interior loops are calculated according to the tables below.

Based on this free energy calculation, an optimal alignment is obtained recursively. Dynamic programming technique is also used to reduce an amount of calculation.

Applications: Some biological reactions occur by base pairing of two sequences. For example, translation initiation site is recognized by base pairing of 16S rRNA and Shine-Dalgarno sequence in bacteria, You can use this tool to characterize base pairings.
