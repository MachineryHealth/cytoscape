    Molecular interaction models provide us with a framework for integrating the large-scale data that we are now able to collect at multiple levels of biological information - genes, RNAs, proteins, and small molecules. Cell Circuits is a web-based interface for searching for genes that appear in our library of network models.

Features

Search the network models in Cell Circuits ...
  • for yeast (S. cerevisiae), fly (D. melanogaster), worm (C. elegans), human (H. sapiens), or malaria parasite (P. falciparum) genes by symbol or alias as registered by the Gene Ontology database
  • using asterisk (*) as a wild card (for gene queries only)
  • for multiple genes at once (space delimited)
  • for models statistically enriched for Gene Ontology terms

Examples

(try entering words in bold into search box)
  • Yeast models: AAP1, GCN*
  • Worm models: CG2849
  • Models enriched for GO term: GO:0003677
  • Models enriched for GO terms containing words (enclose in double quotes): "DNA binding"
